The Best Asian Dramas in my opinions

My top 3 dramas of all-time.

F4 Thailand Boys Over Flower

Its a really good adaptation of the korean Version "Boys over Flower". The ML and Fl potrayed the story in a really good way. It is a great blend of Romance and comedy with a very very almost non existent love triangle which i am soo thankful for

Love like a galaxy

This Cdrama is my number 1 rewatch of chinese dramas despite the fact that it makes we go through a rollercoaster of emotions the cdrama portrays a story of a young immature girl who matures significantly as the drama goes on

Our beloved Summer

Super beautiful Kdrama. Really artistic and very realistic representation of relationships Super funny with serious themes as well
